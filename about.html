Our Company

The Prasfutan Family is all about boosting kids' creativity worldwide! We believe every child has unique smarts - like word smarts (reading, writing, talking), number smarts (calculating, reasoning), picture smarts (drawing, visualizing), body smarts (building, acting), music smarts (singing, rhyming), people smarts (teaching, learning together), and self smarts (thinking, understanding oneself).

We think future champs will be super smart in many ways, blending science with drama or music, like those showing how science affects our lives. Writing creatively, especially poetry, uses both sides of your brain - language and logic, plus visualization and creativity. Employers love folks who think with their whole brain, and being a creative problem solver is top-notch. It's about seeing things differently, finding unique solutions! Poetry, drama, singing, dancing, and art help us remember stuff and get super creative, preparing us to tackle challenges and look ahead.

Prasfutan Family is all about uncovering and boosting kids' talents for a brighter tomorrow
