NCLF
(National Children Literature Festival)

The National Children Literature Festival, an annual event organized by "Prasfutan Family" and in collaboration
with various stakeholders, aims to showcase diverse forms of art from children across the nation.
This 3-day festival, scheduled for February 2024, will encompass literature, art, music, drama, dance, photography, film,
sculpture, painting, and documentaries, symbolizing the essence of "Navarasa."
